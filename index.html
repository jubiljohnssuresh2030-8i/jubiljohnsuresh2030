WHAT IS INTRODUCTION TO PROGRAMMING?

Introduction to programming involves learning to instruct a computer to perform tasks by writing a sequence of commands, called a program, in a specific language.This process, also known as coding, requires understandingfundamental concepts like syntax (the rules of the language), variables (containers for data), and operations (actions on data). The goal is to translate an algorithm or a solution to a problem into a language that a computer can execute, ultimately producing a desired output.
