Problem 1: Print Multiplication Table Problem Statement: Write an algorithm and pseudocode to print the multiplication table of a number entered by the user. Example Input: Number = 6 Expected Output:
6 × 1 = 6

6 × 2 = 12
…
6 × 10 = 60
(Use a for loop from 1 to 10) Problem



ALGORITHM

STEP 1:START

STEP 2: DISPLAY ”ENTER THE NUMBER”

STEP 3: INPUT N

STEP 4: FOR ( i=1, i <=10, i++)

STEP 5: PRODUCT ← N*i

STEP 6: DISPLAY N , ”*” , i , “=” , PRODUCT

STEP 7: STOP

PSEUDOCODE

Begin

Use variables :N, i of type integer

Display ”Enter the Number”

Accept N

FOR ( i=1, i<=10, i++)

Product= N*i

Display N, “*” , i , “=”, Product

END FOR

End program
